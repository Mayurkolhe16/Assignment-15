India Spiritual and Religious Texts


Bhagavad Gita

Bhagavad Gita is a scripture based on the Mahabharata's sermon to Arjuna by Shri Krishna. It contains 18 chapters and 700 Sanskrit verses. This book is an important source for considering various aspects of life.

  • कर्मण्येवाधिकारस्ते मा फलेषु कदाचन। मा कर्मफलहेतुर्भूर्मा ते सङ्गोऽस्त्वकर्मणि॥

Mahabharata

The Mahābhārata is an ancient Indian epic that narrates the great Kurukshetra War between two groups of cousins: the Kauravas and the Pandavas.

  • The Pandavas, led by the righteous Yudhishthira, ultimately emerge victorious, but the war leads to the destruction of human purity and gives rise to envy, greed, and arrogance

Ramayana

The Ramayana is an ancient Hindu epic that tells the story of Rama, the seventh incarnation of the god Vishnu.

  • Rama, along with his wife Sita and brother Lakshmana, embarks on a fourteen-year exile to the forest after being urged by his father, King Dasharatha.During their journey, Sita is kidnapped by the demon king Ravana, leading to a great war, and eventually, Rama’s triumphant return to Ayodhya as king
